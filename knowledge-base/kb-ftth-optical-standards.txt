FTTH Optical Power Standards:
- Standard compliance threshold for ONT/ONU optical power levels: -28.0 dBm to -8.0 dBm (inclusive).
- Ideal optical power levels: -22.0 dBm to -15.0 dBm.
- Warning threshold (Optical level low): -27.9 dBm to -25.0 dBm.
- Failure threshold (Optical power too low): below -28.0 dBm (requires technician to clean connector or verify splitters).
- Failure threshold (Optical power too high): above -8.0 dBm (requires attenuation).
